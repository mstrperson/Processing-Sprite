Place your image files in this folder.

The main sketch expects:
  hero.png  — your player character image
  tree.png  — a decoration image (or rename/replace with whatever you like)

Tips:
  - PNG files with transparent backgrounds look best
  - Any size works — Processing will scale the image to whatever size you specify
  - Recommended free resources:
      opengameart.org   — game sprites, free to use
      remove.bg         — remove the background from any photo (free, online)
