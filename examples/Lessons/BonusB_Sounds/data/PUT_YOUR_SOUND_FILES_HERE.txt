Place your sound files in this folder.

The main sketch expects:
  collect.wav   — a short sound effect for collecting a coin (WAV recommended)
  gameover.wav  — a sound for when the game ends
  music.mp3     — background music that will loop (MP3 or WAV)

Tips:
  - WAV files are the most reliable format in Processing
  - Keep sound effects short (under 2 seconds)
  - Lower the music volume in code with bgMusic.amp(0.4) so effects can be heard
  - Recommended free resources:
      freesound.org       — thousands of free sound effects (free account required)
      opengameart.org     — free game music and sounds
      zapsplat.com        — free sound effects (free account required)
